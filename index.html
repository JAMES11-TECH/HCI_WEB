RideXpress

RideXpress is a ride-sharing service that offers convenient and affordable land transportation options. We connect passengers with drivers through a mobile app, allowing users to request rides, track their driver's location, and pay for their trips digitally. RideXpress aims to provide a safe, reliable, and hassle-free alternative to traditional taxi services.
